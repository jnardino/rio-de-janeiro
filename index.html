Why Rio de Janeiro?

Land of Samba and natural beauty

Rio de Janeiro never fails to impress us with its modern outlook that reflects its progression through the times of yore. The historic sites, sparkling beaches, green belts and cheerful attitude of the locals attract tourists where heaven meets earth.

The rain-forests, museums, beaches gardens and the glitz of the city have made Rio what it is today - Brazil's top-notch tourist destination.
